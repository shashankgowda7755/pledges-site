Sparrow Quiz: Questions and Answers

Question 1: What is the primary diet of sparrows?
Options: A) Seeds & insects, B) Fruits & nuts, C) Fish, D) Leaves
Answer: A) Seeds & insects

Question 2: What sound does a sparrow typically make?
Options: A) Quack, B) Hoot, C) Chirp, D) Caw
Answer: C) Chirp

Question 3: How long do sparrows typically live in the wild?
Options: A) 1–2 years, B) 3–5 years, C) 6–10 years, D) 11–15 years
Answer: B) 3–5 years

Question 4: What is a group of sparrows called?
Options: A) Flock, B) School, C) Herd, D) Swarm
Answer: A) Flock

Question 5: What is a baby sparrow called?
Options: A) Kitten, B) Pup, C) Chick, D) Cub
Answer: C) Chick

Question 6: How do sparrows communicate with each other?
Options: A) Feather display, B) Dances, C) Beak taps, D) Songs and calls
Answer: D) Songs and calls

Question 7: How many species of sparrows are there?
Options: A) About 50, B) About 100, C) About 200, D) About 300
Answer: C) About 200

Question 8: What is the most common species of sparrow?
Options: A) House Sparrow, B) Song Sparrow, C) Tree Sparrow, D) Savannah Sparrow
Answer: A) House Sparrow

Question 9: What's the average weight of a House Sparrow?
Options: A) 10–15 grams, B) 20–25 grams, C) 30–35 grams, D) 40–45 grams
Answer: B) 20–25 grams

Question 10: How far can sparrows travel from their nest to find food?
Options: A) 100 meters, B) 500 meters, C) 1 kilometer, D) 5 kilometers
Answer: B) 500 meters

Question 11: What season do sparrows molt their feathers?
Options: A) Spring, B) Summer, C) Winter, D) Fall
Answer: D) Fall

Question 12: What colour are the legs of a House Sparrow?
Options: A) Blue, B) Red, C) Brown, D) Yellow
Answer: C) Brown

Question 13: What type of sparrow has a black patch on its chest?
Options: A) Tree Sparrow, B) House Sparrow, C) Song Sparrow, D) Savannah Sparrow
Answer: B) House Sparrow

Question 14: What is the wingspan of an average House Sparrow?
Options: A) 10–12 cm, B) 13–15 cm, C) 16–18 cm, D) 19–21 cm
Answer: C) 16–18 cm

Question 15: What is the scientific name of the House Sparrow?
Options: A) Passer domesticus, B) Passer montanus, C) Melospiza melodia, D) None of the above
Answer: A) Passer domesticus

Question 16: What feature distinguishes male and female House Sparrows?
Options: A) Colour of feathers, B) Beak size, C) Leg length, D) Tail shape
Answer: A) Colour of feathers

Question 17: How long does it take for sparrow eggs to hatch?
Options: A) 3–5 days, B) 6–8 days, C) 11–14 days, D) 15–20 days
Answer: C) 11–14 days

Question 18: What is the typical clutch size for a sparrow?
Options: A) 1–2 eggs, B) 3–7 eggs, C) 8–10 eggs, D) 11–15 eggs
Answer: B) 3–7 eggs

Question 19: Where are sparrows commonly found in India?
Options: A) Assam, B) Kashmir, C) South India & Assam, D) Mostly South
Answer: C) South India & Assam

Question 20: How many eggs do sparrows usually lay?
Options: A) 5–6, B) 4–5, C) 6–8, D) 3–4
Answer: B) 4–5
